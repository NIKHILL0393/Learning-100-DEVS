Deep Dish Brownies

Prep Time

60 mins

Servings

9

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter,melted
  • 1 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees f(175 degrees) and grease an 8 inch square pan
  2. In a large bowl, blend melted butter,sugar and vanilla
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time
  4. combine the flour,cocoa,baking powder and salt. gradually blend into the egg mixture
  5. Spread th batter into the prepared pan
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 40 to 45 min or until brownies begin to pull away from ther sides of the pan
  7. Let brownies cool, then cut into squares, enjoy

Tip

Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly and make clean up easier
